# Sources — scripture and belief

Scripture's master copies live in the writing project (`~/penny-one/projects/writing/qelan/`), where Hannu edits them and new drafts land — `absorb sources` reads the masters when that folder exists. The `sources/` folder here is a checked-in mirror of the same documents, so an instance carries its scripture with it: `tools/publish.sh` refreshes the mirror from the masters; in a fork, the mirror *is* the scripture.

| Source | Status | What it is |
|---|---|---|
| `sources/cosmology.md` | **true** | The worldbible: the Ladder of Degrees, the séqa, iron, the four ages and the Rekindling, the three doors. Physics — play never contradicts it. |
| `sources/language.md` | **true** | Sahíric (Lešān-Šahír): degree templates, radiant aspect, the `men-` inversion, core lexicon, the rayed script. |
| `sources/index.html` (also `sources/The-Codex-of-Degrees.pdf`, `sources/codex-print.html`) | **believed** | *The Codex of Degrees*, Iren Sahrwright, Lector of Degrees, Long Quiet 812. What the age knows and teaches: confident, careful, wrong at the seams. The gap between it and the worldbible is the GM's playground. |
| `sources/assets/` | — | Qenah script specimens and titling font. |

**A note for the Mode-A player:** the *true* documents are the GM's physics. Reading them is your choice; the game is better if the Codex is what you know.

**Incoming drafts.** Rough scenes and fragments land anywhere in the qelan writing folder (a `drafts/` subfolder keeps scripture clean, but any file works). On `absorb sources`, new material is triaged: *true* / *believed* / **draft** (see `engine/WORLD.md` §Precedence — drafts bind nothing; they are quarry for play).
