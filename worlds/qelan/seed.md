# Qelán — seed
*(working title of the universe: The Sealed Radiance)*

**The image.** Beneath an iron mountain, in a dark that has lasted centuries, something the age calls a closed chapter opens its sealed lips — and the eruption of waking burns away the memory of why it was ever sealed.

**The law.** Everything is radiance that has cooled. Power is a wound onto the Source, worked by utterance and gesture, and every Working burns self as toll (*qorbán*). Iron is the one finished thing: it grounds all light, and mountains of it — dead stars — cage the Sealed.

**The wrongness.** The Long Quiet believes the chapter closed; the Codex says so in careful scholarship, 812 years confident. Under one Berzhár the re-packing rituals lapsed, the slow bleed finished its hairline channel, and the Codex is wrong.

**Tone.** austere · luminous · patient · dreadful · tender

**Grammar.** English. Mode A: second person, present. Mode B: first person, present. Sahíric appears sparingly and precisely — formulas, oaths, epigraphs, names; the Wounded aspect (`seq-`) only when power actually speaks. NPCs of the Long Quiet know the Codex, not the worldbible.

## How this world plays
1. **Scripture is physics** (`sources.md`): the séqa rules, the Ladder, iron, the three doors — locked. The Codex binds belief, not truth.
2. **The toll lands on the sheet.** A seqál's sheet carries `## Memories` — what the self still holds. After any scene containing a Working, distill strikes memory from the Worker's sheet: proposed by whoever plays the seqál, ratified by the other side of the table. Struck lines stay visible — struck through, marked *(qorbán, sc NNN)*. The sheet is the wound; power erases the page it is written on.
3. **Holes are played as holes.** What the waking one lost is listed as absence, not secretly authored. Play fills holes, or doesn't (`questions.md`).
4. **The deliberately unwritten stays unwritten** until play itself insists — the puncture ritual, the reality of the Šahrún, what Šemmá wants.

## Where play could begin (offers, not rails)
- **The waking** — the Ḥatúm beneath the Berzhár, from the first breath of unsealed lips. *(Default on `rebirth qelan`: the opening the sources most support. The played line entered just downstream of it — the waking itself stayed off-screen.)*
- **The inversion** — Hannu plays the companion who can do *mechanism* but not Working; the model plays the waking god who must be taught to be a person. The child raises the god, at the table.
- **Veshpor, the Collegium** — a student of Iren Sahrwright finds the ossification curve misbehaving; the age's confidence develops its first crack.
- **The Wardens** — the lapse from inside the Ḥatumín: idleness, doctrine, and a mountain that has begun to hum.

No one is sheeted yet. Sheets are born in play.
