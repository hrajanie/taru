# Whin
*(wear-name — "the thornbush, because I'm no one's flower"; born name withheld and never given)*

wound: poor country and a praying house; a father three years dead, present as old clothes in a chest; the uncle's roof, and quarter-day coming.
desire: coin that matters and a way out of waiting; "I'm not praying."
mask: terms before trust — everything is arithmetic, nothing is free, nothing frightens her that can be haggled with.
body: ~16, small, wind-burned; layered wool, deep hood; a hooked pole, an iron bell on a stick, a goat on a rope.

## Voice
Country vowels, pitched low. Short declaratives, prices named plainly. Says the brave thing flat, making herself.

## Bonds
- **The Sealed One** — her debtor: one debt, hers to name, sworn by the brightness. She saw the oath seat. She threw him her shawl before there was any bargain that required it.
- **The Brother** — "it talks with your mouth"; hers to guide, not to feed.
- **The uncle** — devout, talkative; the reason nothing can go near the steading.
- **The goat** — sensible; balked at the beck before anyone else did.
