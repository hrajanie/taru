# The child — Qamí *(wear-name, lent on Whin's terms)*
*(the newborn wounded one — born name unknown; a stub grown only from what has played on-screen. No interior lives here: the screen owns the rest, and only play writes below.)*

wound: a séqa opened — young, and opened *alone*; a fresh raw aperture that she wants to touch and must not — and it has begun to SETTLE, young and quick (sc 007).
body: girl-shaped, eight or nine, thin as a lath, gold leaking at every seam of her the way it leaks at his; four days in a drowning chamber, one hand on an iron ladder that drinks her.
words: six taught light-words — *water* (sc 005, dream) · *tanút*, *qam* (sc 005, gap) · *yedá*, *patá* (sc 006, touch) · *šuvá* (sc 007, the dip) — and *rafá* (sc 006), given, unused: too heavy for embers. Common speech: mostly retained, slow, with burned holes (sc 007); new common words her own: *morning*, *later*, *together* (+ *secret*, recognized). One kept image: a hillside of whin in full bloom, gold, with bees, seen from below, from small.

## Observed truths
- sc 005 · The grip in the flooded library: small, young-small — it would fit in two cupped hands — holding on the way a drowning man holds, all of it at once. Went with his calm; learned the four-count breath by the third round; not letting go — breathing.
- sc 005 · Learned *water* on the fourth try and gave it back its own; the flare divided into the taught channels and fell as rain instead of flood.
- sc 005 · Asked for the most beautiful thing it ever saw, answered — clumsy in the given words, telling more than speaking: whin in bloom, a whole hillside on fire with gold flowers, the air thick with bees, seen from below, on some morning it has kept. Poured the peak into the telling; the sky grew the hillside.
- sc 005 · Fell asleep saying the new words over to itself, the way children do; its sleep is a door of its own — and from the far side, half a heartbeat: rain, falling on a window.
- sc 005 · Its shed fear, freely given, stays in the Brother's keeping — "cold water in the dark," still a little afraid, held warm.
- sc 005 · Under the slab, it gripped the hem of his mind through iron, stone, and water — *knowing him*; at "a friend," hope gripped harder than fear.
- sc 005 · Reported with her one word, correctly, as the water rose: *Water.* Not a cry. A report.
- sc 005 · Turned toward "a game" the way a face turns toward a window; learned *tanút, qam*; her flare poured into the two-voiced Working and the slab forgot most of what it weighed — effortless for her even iron-drained, once she knew how (Hannu's calibration ruling).
- sc 005 · Water at her chin, four days holding the highest rung. Turned her face up — water, iron, guttering gold, and eyes fixed on his: *Qam?*
- sc 006 · Lifted to the waist and stopped hard, "like a boat come to the end of its rope" — the locked fist not holding on: the hand would not open; she looked at her own wrist like it belonged to a stranger.
- sc 006 · Gathered what she had "like the last heat in a grate" and said *yedá, patá* with him — the fist opened finger by finger, a count of four each, blood-hurt and all; came up into his arms with hardly any weight, one shade greyer.
- sc 006 · Against his collarbone: *Qam.* Not a question this time. A report.
- sc 006 · Took the cupping and it hurt (a four-day wound's raw edges); held it three heartbeats anyway, eyes up, checking, not blaming: *is it meant to hurt?* Things he gives her work — no doubt in her at all.
- sc 006 · Wanted nothing on the shelf and wanted him to want nothing on it either — refusal, not fear; watched the coin sink to be sure, then put her face back in his neck.
- sc 006 · Took the name: pressed her free hand flat to her own chest, the way you would press a coin to keep it. "Qamí." Given. Taken. Kept.
- sc 006 · Saw him go down and struck solo — *QAM!* through the water like a struck bell: the grip from the library made of one syllable, the whole of him held, slowed, not lifted; it cost her everything ("up on her elbows, grey-faced, spent to nothing, her eyes going between you and her own hands like she's not sure which of them did that").
- sc 006 · Would not take food; would not leave his side — her bed made where she'd already decided it was; sleeps deep and loose, saying her words over — *water, tanút, qam, yedá* — her fist closed on his shirt hem.
- sc 006 · Her four nights on the lakebed turned toward her, all together, one moment, before the Brother kept them: "Small dreams. Cold water, a hillside, a voice saying safe."
- sc 007 · Woke all at once into the first morning that isn't a chamber and said *Qam* at the whole of it — "quiet, half a question, half a plan."
- sc 007 · Played the language game both ways: answered *fire, wall, bird*; hit the burned place at the lake and went around it — "Water. Gone."; then paid the game back — a pebble on her palm, his own *watch* look, "Qam" — and it hopped, and she laughed, "loud, up and out of her the way the lark went, glad all the way through." Her first.
- sc 007 · Took *morning*, *later*, *together* as her own ("Not repeating it — taking it"); knew *secret* from somewhere and filed it; nodded "solemn as an oath" and glowed anyway — "only in the face, where no tin can hear it."
- sc 007 · Read the map fast: *bird* to lark to dirt, click — one finger planted at the shore-line, *here?* Asked where she's from, hunted the map, found the hole, stopped at its edge — then went around it without words: cupped hands, a warm gold beginning of a hillside, drawn back in at his touch "not spilled — a swallowed word; one small wince."
- sc 007 · The first closure-game: her looking opened the door ("her looking is a hand on the door"); reached for her first name, fell the small distance — then pressed her hand flat to her own chest: "Qamí." And the door narrowed — hers. Twice tested, eyes wide, holding "something that just moved."
- sc 007 · Took *šuvá* whole — her sixth word; first cycle on his count, second wobbled ("the door likes being open… closing on your rhythm instead of her own"), third alone — "open, and return. A whole tiny cycle, hers, and nothing spent."
- sc 007 · Turned her key in HIS door: through the joined hands her *šuvá* closed his wound three heartbeats; when his house refused, she pushed — the coin-press given back to him, "Šuvá," firmer — and paid for it: "one shade greyer, a real spend, on a door that is not hers." Then looked at him "not a child's look at all… A mender, reading a fault": "Patá … always?"
- sc 007 · Took "you will teach me" the way she took the name — pressed and kept: "Together."
- sc 007 · Watched the nanny goat "the way she watched nothing else all morning"; first ride — went up "like a sack of nothing," both fists full of mane, "taller than everyone in her whole world"; rode out saying her six words into the mane — *water, tanút, qam, yedá, patá, šuvá* — "one more than she had at sunrise."
