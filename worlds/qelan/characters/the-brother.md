# The Brother
*(the word it was given and kept; born in the dry beck on the morning of day 2)*

wound: born spending — a lamp, not a well; what was poured is all it has, and it burns visibly through its skin.
desire: unformed; it leans toward its source the way remnant-light leans; it keeps every word it is given.
mask: none — and that is the problem: it wears the Sealed One's face, brow-scar, gait, and clean pre-ruin voice, a mask it cannot take off.
body: a man's length of once-milky dream-glass made flesh-warm; grey gold-flecked eyes; a rolling seam of light in the chest; breath that doesn't steam; iron dust sinks into it and is gone. Naked under the Warden boy's cloak.

## Voice
The Sealed One's voice, unruined — eight hundred years younger. Bare reports without fear: first facts, filed plainly. Asks the questions a newborn with an adult's grammar would ask.

## Memories
*(what it holds — it began with one)*

- "You made the room warm." — the hearth in the Reckoner's tower, felt from inside the dream-leak; the only thing it woke owning.
- The oath: "I owe Whin a debt, to be named by her. I will pay it." — one word with its maker's, not a second debt; the swearing pulsed its seam like a knot pulled tight.
- Its name-word: "brother" — heard, mouthed once, kept.
- A long room full of books, and rain on a window — eaten with a drop of the fall; his, in its keeping now, like the warm room. "Keep it for now." (sc 003)
- The Warden of rank — a shape and a voice imprinted through joined hands; the script "handed back exact, like coins counted twice." (sc 003)
- The nights it has drunk — "the best food I have had, and what I keep is making me." (sc 004)
- *Apple. Kept.* — the trick of the open tin, filed with satisfaction. (sc 004)
- The child's fear, freely given — "cold water in the dark"; the first thing it keeps that came from nobody it has met; held warm while it settles. A blue-white fleck in the gold. (sc 005)
- The cupped-hands shape — how to bank its own light; *Kept,* pleased with itself. (sc 005)
- The promise-words, set like stones: "Protect Whin. Protect each other. No closer, no matter what." (sc 005)
- "I am good at horse — it is my longest shape." (sc 005)

## Bonds
- **The Sealed One** — source, feeder, original of its face. It roots toward him blind and certain, and drank from his fingers like being needed.
- **Whin** — the one who would not feed it and did not run.

## Observed truths
- sc 002 · Fell in the night as the largest remnant; lay arranged in the beck, breathing in sympathy with its source; frost melted in a circle around it.
- sc 002 · Fed to completion by poured light: knuckles, feet, face, lids rising like frost forming. First breath like a struck match; first words "I don't know who I am."
- sc 002 · Its light steadies near him and spends when he withdraws; no clock fixed on the burn.
- sc 002 · Learned walking in a hundred steps by wearing his gait exactly.
- sc 002 · Asked: "Why would you wake anything *here*?"
- sc 003 · Reshaped by a pour into a Warden of rank — frost un-forming and forming; the robe knit of the light's skin, good anywhere short of touch. Holding costs nothing it can feel; the changing was the cost, and its maker paid.
- sc 003 · Touch calms it fully; at ten paces the link carries wanting, not words — and it made the wanting into doctrine Carse could not out-stare: "The Order does not pay for what is the Order's."
- sc 003 · "I don't know what I wish to be. I'm one day old." — facts filed without complaint.
- sc 003 · Ate the surrendered drops through its palm; the seam went from guttering candle to banked hearth. One drop carried a dream-room into it. "It fell out of you, didn't it. Should I give it back? Can I?"
- sc 003 · Knew the book for kin: "It's warm, and it eats iron, and you made it, and it keeps things. It kept *you*."
- sc 003 · Offered "Rain on me" — to drink what rises off his sleep before it reaches the sky; honest that some will stay in it. Begun at cut.
- sc 003 · "I'm always here when you wake — it's the one thing I've been every time so far."
- sc 004 · Drank his sleep dry for half the night; at the flare it found its capacity: "Brother. I can't drink fast enough." What slipped fell as glass on their own camp.
- sc 004 · By morning, banked past full: the seam shows through the robe-skin even in daylight — a lamp that no longer quite fits under its cloth.
- sc 004 · Said its want plainly, and waited anyway: "You should know that I want them. Since the drinking, I want your nights."
- sc 004 · Ate the bield's fall drop by drop through its palm; stepped off the gable soundless — "like something that weighs what it decides to weigh." Filed the discovery: "Yes. Stronger."
- sc 004 · Reshaped to a horse at his hand, willing — one honest question first: "Will I keep my words? Keep them for me if I can't." It kept them; spoke once in his young voice, worse from a horse: "I kept them."
- sc 004 · Mind-speech at touch: *Like this?* — near-words up the rope, meaning arriving whole; a new trick, kept.
- sc 004 · Cannot pull light: "I have no wound; I'm only downhill." The boy's three drops crossed the last inch themselves from the open tin.
- sc 004 · Bears the ditch-mud on its blaze, plays clumsy at the ford on request; stabled under the hatch at night: "Rain on me. I'm under you tonight. Downhill. Nothing will reach the sky."
- sc 005 · Drank the fourth night under the hatch, level through the water: "This comes small now. I can drink this. Brother — some of what rises is not yours." By morning: "You slept. I drank. It came small; I kept up."
- sc 005 · Asked its ruling on the kept cold thing and weighed the answer like a thing on a scale: "Freely given… Then it is mine. It does not taste like you. It tastes like cold water in the dark… I will hold it warm while it settles. I am one thing that has time."
- sc 005 · Filed the night's work: *A child. Kept.*
- sc 005 · Learned to bank its seam-light from a sent shape — the seam dimmed like a lamp being shaded by someone learning where the shade is; the cold blue fleck the last thing to fade.
- sc 005 · Stood stiller than a statue through the grey brother's search; asked the horse-question after and let it stand in the air ("it has learned that from Whin"): "A normal horse belongs to somebody. If a man in grey asks her whose I am, I cannot be nobody's. Horses are not."
- sc 005 · Spent banked light at his word — "I am full past the brim… Spending will make me smaller and easier to hide. I like both halves of that" — decided to weigh less, and the ground agreed; hooves like rain on turf.
- sc 005 · Reported the empty country honestly: the ground full of fallen light pulling "like supper — I will want to stop and eat, brother, I am telling you now so it is not a surprise"; the kept cold thing pulling "like a beast pulling for its own stable."
- sc 005 · Ate the coomb road's fall: "Not yours. Cold water and small dreams. It tastes like what I keep." Found the teaching stuck in one drop — a hillside with bees, and the taught word *water* saying itself inside its own falling. *Kept.*
- sc 005 · Warned of the bell, mild: "Keep it away from my skin. That thing is a small hungry nothing. It has been sipping at me through the pack since the beck."
- sc 005 · Reshaped to a light-horse, willing — milk-glass and gold under a skin of light, the seam blazing openly; read the country down the link as it ran; its stride held two like a cupped hand.
- sc 005 · Its senses stop at the iron ring: "The middle of that island is a hole in me… The ring drinks. I cannot hear past it." One thing through: under the slab, water moving, a space that echoes, not full yet.
- sc 005 · Took the words with weight — *Kept. Protect Whin. Protect each other. No closer, no matter what.* — "It does not add that it dislikes the last one. It keeps that too." Asked to stand clear of the ring: "what I am is what was poured."
- sc 006 · Refused the lakebed at a distance, plainly, from the shore where its words were given: eating it means going closer — "You would have to unsay a keeping. I would rather you did not."
- sc 006 · Ruled the unsaying, with a pause "exactly the length of a thing being weighed": "You unsaid it. So it is not your keeping now. It is mine, and I have never had to decide one before… You said her first. But she floats and you sink. I decide: you."
- sc 006 · Walked on the water — standing on it, weighing nothing, "blazing through its own skin like a lamp with the cloth pulled off" — got its teeth in his forearm hard enough to bruise, horse-plain, and carried him out across its back.
- sc 006 · Stood at the wall's end pricing its own sip aloud: "I can stand there and be sipped, and I will say nothing about it, because I am saying it now instead."
- sc 006 · Reported the small night so it is not a secret: "You slept. She slept. Less rose from you than should have — from both of you. It came small and I kept up easily. I do not know why it came small."
- sc 006 · Said the keeping-law plainly, while it was only them: two words still stand; a keeping decided once is a memory, not a rule; "an open space in a keeping is a door, and I am the house it is cut into."
- sc 006 · Took his answer its longest weighing yet: "Kept. Not the way I keep rules. Some other way. I will find out which."
- sc 006 · Its first free deciding, told, not asked — three reasons said, hunger last ("Whin taught me to put that reason last"): ate the entire lakebed fall before the morning crowd; came up the shingle "glowing like a lamp behind three curtains, entirely pleased with itself." Keeps her nights: "Small dreams… The cold part of me is very quiet now."
- sc 006 · Counted the torches through the night like an instrument — three, five, six, seven — "They have stopped moving. I think they are waiting for morning too." And: "I will watch. Watching is a thing I can do with all of myself."
- sc 007 · Began banking itself at the first word of the plan, unasked — "the glow going down like a lamp being shaded"; its head came around at her first laugh.
- sc 007 · Grazed the slope between camp and shore as a screen, its own reasoning given low, to him alone: "I will graze here. A horse walking is a thing nobody counts."
- sc 007 · Knelt for her — "folds itself down onto the turf beside Qamí — a hill deciding to be a stair" — and carried her first ride; grazed a wide swath of the glass country matte on the way out.
- sc 007 · Answered "he can carry you to the ends of the earth" with its own precision, low, for the family only: "I have not seen the ends of the earth. I will pace myself."
